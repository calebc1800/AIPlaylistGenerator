## Overview

AI Playlist Generator is a web application that empowers users to create personalized music playlists using AI prompts and connect them directly to their favorite music streaming services like Spotify. Whether you're looking for the perfect workout mix, a chill study session soundtrack, or discovering new artists, our AI-powered platform helps you create the perfect playlist in seconds.

## Usage

### Creating Your First AI Playlist

1. **Sign in** with your music streaming account
2. **Enter a prompt** describing your desired playlist:
   - "Upbeat indie rock for morning commute"
   - "Relaxing jazz for studying"
   - "90s hip-hop throwback party mix"
3. **Review** the AI-generated playlist
4. **Customize** by adding or removing tracks
5. **Save** and sync to your music service
6. **Share** with friends or the community

### Discovering Playlists

- Browse **Top Rated** playlists from the community
- Explore **Friends' Playlists** for personalized recommendations
- Search by **mood**, **genre**, or **activity**
